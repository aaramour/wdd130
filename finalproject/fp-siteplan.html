Final Project Site Plan

Site Purpose

Last night I was ready to unwind and just watch some TV. I logged in to Netflix and I felt like I tried to find what to watch for a longer time than I actually watched something. And what I did watch wasn’t even any good! Anyway, I’ve got this idea to build a website. You’ll tell it what streaming services you have and it will make recommendations based on internet reviews. If you’ve seen it already, you mark “seen it” (or “love” or “hate”). You can also filter by genres and things like adult content. With a site like this, we could spend time watching rather than spending time blindly choosing!

Intended Target Audience

  • 1. Those who want to check which movies/shows are on which services.
  • 2. Those who want to browse movies/shows by genre/actor/rating etc
  • 3. Those who want movie/show recommendations given to them
  • 4. Those who want to track and rate what they watch and/or build a watch lists

Site Map and Content

This site will allow users to search movies and TV shows first and foremost. On the main page there will be a search bar to search titles. There will be a way to filter searches by movie/show, by rating (PG, R, etc), and filters by genre, and by duration (binge watch over the weekend vs a movie right now). By default the user will have entered which streaming services they have, but there will be an easy way to check and uncheck services and to only show my services or show all.

The Search Results page will show the results from the search. Each movie/show will have its own page once selected. This page will provide an extremely brief rundown of each movie with Genres, Rating, Ranking, and 2 streaming services.

The page for each movie/show will display more content than the search results page. It will have brief rundowns of ratings, rankings, genres, streaming services, and related films/shows.
