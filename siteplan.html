Site Name

Dry Oar Rafting Company

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to be the center of information for the consumer interested in adventurously exploring the St. Johns River in Jacksonville, FL. The information contained in will answer any questions the consumer may have, such as how much a trip will cost and what kind of equipment or gear will need to be rented and/or purchased. The site will allow the consumer to purchase an expedition package and any gear required. The site will have a section for frequently asked questions and contact information for more specific questions. The site will also have a section for purchase of branded memorabilia and equipment for purchase in order to increase revenue. Ultimately the site is intended to be the place where a consumer can discover Dry Oar and book a trip the same day. The site will answer questions like "What is there to do in North Florida?" and "What does an expedition look like from start to finish as far as time and cost and equipment?" and "What if I am looking for more of a thrill? What's next for me?"

Audience

The audience for this site is going to be young adults looking for a new adventure or parents looking for a memorable family outing in North Florida or South Georgia. There are different expeditions packages available in order to reduce cost for the consumer as much as possible and include someone from all backgrounds. We want to cater to those that are looking for something new to do but also want to provide enough fun for experienced rafters to have a local rafting company to spend their time with. The audience will access the site through mobile device or laptop generally when they are looking for something fun to do or when they already know they want to go rafting in Jacksonville.
